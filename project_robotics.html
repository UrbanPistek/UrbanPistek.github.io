About the Team

FIRST Robotics is a competition that has high school teams building a robot from scratch to preform certain actions in a competition enviroment. Our robot had a tank belt driven powertrain powered by DC electric motors with a robotic arm extending from the frame. The robotic arm was controlled by a chain from a gearbox with torque from 2 DC motors along with gas shocks to dampen the load on the chain. The robot arm was able to further extend forward via a pneumatic pistion and had a sissor grip to grab objects.

About FIRST
